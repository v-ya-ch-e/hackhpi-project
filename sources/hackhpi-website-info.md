- Completely free (challenges, workshops, networking events, meals/snacks)
- Cash prizes for best solutions + best meme of the event

## FAQ Highlights

- **What is a hackathon?** Students come together to collaborate and turn ideas into real projects over two consecutive days on HPI's campus.
- **What's special about HackHPI?** One of few hackathons focusing on social issues (agriculture, climate). Real-world challenges from partners using their APIs and expertise.
- **Costs:** Free. Food, drinks, caffeine provided. Travel scholarships available via QuantCo.
- **Accommodation:** All-night event. Rooms provided for sleeping/resting; bring your own sleeping bag and air mattress.
- **Preparation:** Bring laptop + charger, comfortable clothes, motivation. Introductory sessions provided.
